FAQ

Common questions

What's included in the pricing? +
All prices include design, development, content integration, SEO setup, analytics configuration, and the specified number of revision rounds. You own all the code and content we create.
How long does a project take? +
Starter Launch: 2-3 weeks. Lead-Gen Pro: 4-6 weeks. Performance Plus: 8-12 weeks. Timeline depends on content readiness and feedback cycles.
Do you offer payment plans? +
Yes! We offer flexible payment plans for all packages. Typically 50% upfront, 50% on launch. For Performance Plus, we can split into 3-4 milestones. Contact us to discuss options.
What if I need something custom? +
We love custom projects! Book a strategy call and we'll create a tailored proposal with transparent pricing based on your specific requirements and goals.
Can I switch Care Plans later? +
Absolutely! You can upgrade, downgrade, pause, or cancel your Care Plan anytime with 30 days notice. No lock-in contracts.
Do you work with businesses outside Australia? +
While we specialize in Australian businesses and .au domains, we work with international clients too. Our team operates across Australian and Sri Lankan time zones for extended coverage.
What platforms do you build on? +
We specialize in WordPress (with ACF/Blocks), Headless (Next.js + Node), Shopify for e-commerce, and Webflow where suitable. We recommend the best platform based on your needs and technical requirements.
Do you provide hosting? +
Hosting is included in all Care Plans with Australian CDN POPs for optimal performance. For project-only clients, we provide hosting recommendations and setup assistance.
